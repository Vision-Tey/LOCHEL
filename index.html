We are working hard on three fronts which are about to be presented.

Our Activities are based on our three Objectives listed;

To assist the Needy children, To assist the Elderly and To assist the Orphans

Providing Safe Water to communities

We have carried out water source protection in some communities by rehabilitating the available water reservors and sources with the available resources, clearing and protecting them and this has so far improved people's health and provided safe water for human consumption.

Assisting children with educational requirements

We believe every child deserves a chance to an education and it's our passion to see children and the communities have their potentials unlocked through our work and we have tried this by providing scholastic materials to a number of people in our support community.
